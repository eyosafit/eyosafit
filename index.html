01. The Learning Curve

My Dev Journey

From compiling C++ at 16 to building full-stack platforms and 3D games.

Age 16

The C++ Genesis

It all started with C++. I spent 6 intense months learning the absolute core of programming, understanding memory, logic, and how machines actually think.

Year 1

The Python Romance

Fell completely in love with Python. Within a year, I became highly proficient, diving into GUI development with Kivy and KivyMD. Built cross-platform desktop video players for Linux/Windows and a full mobile delivery app.

Year 2

The Full-Stack Sprint

Mastered modern web dev (Django, Flask, Tailwind, JS) in an intense 4-month sprint. Scaled my logic to the web, building live multiplayer games like Bingo, Ludo, Casino, and Snake & Ladder architectures.

Year 3

Teaching & Video Editing

Started a YouTube channel to teach others. Realizing attention is everything, I mastered CapCut, blending motion graphics, pacing, and cinematic storytelling to create high-retention tutorials.

Current (Year 4)

The Godot Era

Transitioned into professional game engines. Spent the last 6 months mastering Godot 4. Developed slick platformers and massive 3D FPS Arena games, including my flagship project inspired by The Maze Runner.
